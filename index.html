About Us

Welcome to FramFresh

FramFresh is a simple e-commerce website that allows users to browse and purchase products online. Our website features a clean and intuitive design, making it easy for users to find and buy the products they need.

Features:

Product Browse :- Browse our collection of products, each with a detailed description, price, and image.
Add to Cart :- Easily add products to your cart with a single click.
View Cart :- View the contents of your cart, including the quantity and total price of each item.
Custom Quantity :- Select a custom quantity for each product before adding it to your cart.

Future Development:

Payment Gateway Integration:- We plan to integrate a payment gateway to allow users to securely checkout and pay for their orders.
User Accounts:- We plan to add user account functionality, allowing users to save their cart contents and order history.
